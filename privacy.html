Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

RSA Passport ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our networking application for events organized under Rotaract South Asia MDIO.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Email address, full name, participant ID
  • Profile Information: District, profession, phone number
  • Authentication Data: Google account information (name, email, profile photo) used for sign-in

2.2 Information Automatically Collected

When you use the Service, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Usage Data: Connection history, QR code scans, points earned, leaderboard rankings
  • Device Information: Device type, browser type, operating system
  • Local Storage: Cached user data stored locally on your device for performance optimization

2.3 Information from Other Users

When other participants scan your QR code, they may view your profile information including name, district, and profile photo.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Authenticate your identity and manage your account
  • Facilitate networking connections between participants
  • Calculate and display leaderboard rankings
  • Generate and manage your QR code
  • Track connection history and scoring
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraudulent activity
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored using Firebase services (Google Cloud Platform):

  • Firestore: Primary database storing user profiles, connection history, and application data
  • Realtime Database: Cached data for performance optimization (leaderboard, ranks, QR codes)
  • Local Storage: Temporary caching on your device for faster access

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 With Other Participants

When you scan or are scanned by another participant, the following information is shared:

  • Your name and profile photo
  • Your district information
  • Your profession (if visible in profile)
  • Connection timestamp

5.2 Public Information

The following information is visible to all authenticated participants:

  • Leaderboard rankings (top participants)
  • Public profile information (name, district, score, rank)

5.3 With Service Providers

We use third-party service providers to help us operate the Service:

  • Google Firebase: Authentication, database, and cloud functions
  • Google Cloud Platform: Hosting and infrastructure services

These service providers have access to your information only to perform services on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

5.4 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the Service and fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Event-related data may be retained for the duration of the event and a reasonable period thereafter for administrative and analytical purposes.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can access your profile information through the Service
  • Correction: You can update your profile information through the Service or by contacting the service administrators
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account by contacting the service administrators
  • Opt-out: You can stop using the Service at any time by logging out

Please note that some information may be retained in our records even after account deletion to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. These countries may have data protection laws that differ from those in your country. By using the Service, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

10. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use local storage and session storage to improve your experience with the Service. These technologies help us:

  • Remember your authentication state
  • Cache data locally for faster loading
  • Maintain your session preferences

You can control local storage through your browser settings, though this may affect Service functionality.

11. Third-Party Services

The Service uses Google Sign-In for authentication. When you sign in with Google, you are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review Google's privacy practices.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

13. Your Consent

By using the Service, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
